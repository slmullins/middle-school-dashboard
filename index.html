How to use this dashboard

Compare — sortable/filterable table of all 13 schools with the essentials (tuition, grades, entrance test, key deadline, application fee).

School Profiles — full detail per school: admissions process, requirements, deadlines, contacts, logistics, and a note on gifted/advanced fit.

Application Checklist — a step-by-step to-do list per school plus a shared cross-school section. Check items off; your progress is saved in this browser (works offline). Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

Shared Tests & Forms — the ISEE, HAIS recommendation forms, the HISD School Choice application, FACTS/TEFA financial aid, and more.

⭐ Note on your priority: gifted / academically advanced

Schools flagged ⭐ G/T fit are the strongest matches for an academically advanced 6th grader. Among public options, T.H. Rogers (Vanguard G/T magnet) and Lanier (Vanguard magnet + IB) require your son to be identified Gifted & Talented in all assessment areas to be eligible for the magnet lottery — so confirming/obtaining G/T identification is a key early step (see the checklist). Among private schools, St. John's, Kinkaid, Awty (IB), and St. Thomas' (classical) are the most academically rigorous.
